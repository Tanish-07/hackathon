FreshDesk App

Many organizations face challenges in managing customer support due to scattered tools, manual processes, and lack of a centralized system. Support teams often struggle to efficiently track tickets, manage customer contacts, and maintain consistency across workflows.

The FreshDesk App addresses these challenges by providing a structured, scalable ticket and contact management solution. Built using Angular modules, services, and observables with a backend API, the application focuses on simplicity, maintainability, and efficiency.

This system replicates the core capabilities of Freshdesk in a simplified form, allowing users to seamlessly manage tickets and contacts through a clean and intuitive interface.

Key Functional Requirements

View and search support tickets Create, update, and delete tickets View and search customer contacts Create, update, and delete contacts Angular modules & services
